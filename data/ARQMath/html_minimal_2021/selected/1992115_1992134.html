1. Your likelihood function is incomplete - you've forgotten to include the fact that θ≥xi for each i. The complete function should be ℒθ⋅∏[θ≥xi], where ℒθ is the function above and [⋅] is the indicator function. 2. In the range θ≥max⁡xi, the corrected function and the original function agree, and you've determined that the likelihood function is positive and strictly decreasing. In the range θ<max⁡xi, it is zero once you include the correction. Ergo, the maximiser is θ*=max⁡xi.

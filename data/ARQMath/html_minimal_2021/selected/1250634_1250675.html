Summation of (NK)

I was working on a math problem that required me to figure out the general summation of (N0)+(N1)+…+(NK).

I know that if k=N, the answer is 2N. But is there an answer for a general k?

For reference, this is the problem:

How many N-bit binary strings contain anywhere from none to N2 zeros (inclusive)? N is even.

The answer involves recognizing that all possible combinations of strings that have zeroes can be represented by (N0)+(N1)+…+(NN), and that representing the answer to the problem (anywhere from 0 to N2 zeroes) would be (N0)+(N1)+…+(NN/2).

Hence my question.

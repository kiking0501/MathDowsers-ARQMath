The Taylor expansion of a given function f⁢(x) centered in a point a∈D, where D is the domain of the function, is given by:

f⁢(x)=∑n=0∞1n!⁢f(n)⁢(a)⁢(x-a)n Where f(n)⁢(a) is the nth derivative at the point of centering.

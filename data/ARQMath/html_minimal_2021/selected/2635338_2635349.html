Your solution is perfect.

Note that n∈ℕ,n⁢mod⁢ 2=0 means nothing else but that n is a nonnegative even number, so that writing n=2⁢m for some m∈ℕ is correct.
And indeed, the original set of words could also be replaced by L={a2⁢m⁢bm∣m∈ℕ}

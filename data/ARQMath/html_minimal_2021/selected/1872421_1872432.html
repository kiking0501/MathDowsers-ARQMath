The problem is that the quadratic formula 12⁢a⁢(-b±b2-4⁢a⁢c) suffers from roundoff if 4⁢a⁢c≪b. In that case the square root is very close to b and when you take the plus sign you get cancellation. Rewriting the parentheses as b⁢(-1+1-4⁢a⁢cb2) is a step. Then you can use the binomial theorem to say 1-4⁢a⁢cb2≈1-2⁢a⁢cb2. Now you can cancel the 1's analytically and be left with-2⁢a⁢cb2. This will not overflow your floats and you have avoided the subtraction error.

Method for Counting the Divisors of a number

I need to find the number of divisors of 600. Is there any other way to solve the problem, apart from writing them down and counting??

The prime factorisation of a number lets you quickly compute the number of divisors.

600=23⋅3⋅52.

So any divisor of 600 will be of the form 2a⋅3b⋅5c where 0≤a≤3, 0≤b≤1, and 0≤c≤2.

But -2, - 1, -10 and so on are also divisors of 600.

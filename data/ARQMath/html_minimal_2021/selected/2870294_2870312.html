Here is another way: if A and B are any two matrices that can be multiplied from both sides then t⁢r⁢(A⁢B)=t⁢r⁢(B⁢A). You can easily prove it using the definition of matrix multiplication. Hence if A⁢B=Im and B⁢A=In then m=t⁢r⁢(Im)=t⁢r⁢(A⁢B)=t⁢r⁢(B⁢A)=t⁢r⁢(In)=n.

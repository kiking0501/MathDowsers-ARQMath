c) Let g a continuous function. By the Stone-Weiertrass theorem, there exist a sequence of polynomials Pn converging uniformly to g on [0,1]. Then f⁢(t)⁢Pn⁢(t)→f⁢(t)⁢g⁢(t) uniformly, and we get that ∫01f⁢(t)⁢Pn⁢(t)⁢𝑑t→∫01f⁢(t)⁢g⁢(t)⁢𝑑t, as as the first integral is Pn⁢(c), and that c∈[0,1], we have ∫01f⁢(t)⁢g⁢(t)⁢𝑑t=g⁢(c).

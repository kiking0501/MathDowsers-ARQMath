You can continue tossing coins even after an overall winner is determined. By the fifth coin-flip there is going to be exactly one person with at least 3 wins and that person is the overall winner. Use [binomial distribution](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Binomial_distribution) to calculate the probabilities of a player having won exactly 3, exactly 4, or exactly 5 of the flips over the course of the series and add those probabilities together. If told that certain outcomes have already happened, then only do a binomial distribution over the still remaining games to play until 5 happen.

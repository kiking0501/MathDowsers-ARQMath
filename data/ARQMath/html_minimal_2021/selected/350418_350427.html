We can write limx→0⁡e-1/x2x=limx→∞⁡xex2 and then use de l'Hospital to obtain limx→0⁡e-1/x2x=limx→∞⁡12⁢x⁢ex2=0.

Not without more attention to detail: limx→∞⁡xex2=limx→0+⁡e-1/x2x, not the original two-sided limit.

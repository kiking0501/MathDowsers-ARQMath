That is all you have to do!

The formula for the nth term, Cn, is just the sum of all the integers from n up to 2⁢n.

So

  • C1=1+2=3
  • C2=2+3+4=9
  • C3=3+4+5+6=18
  • C4=4+5+6+7+8=30

Now try it for n=5 and n=6.

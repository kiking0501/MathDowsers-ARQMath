There is not a general simple way for that.

What we can do by hand is to start from the well known cases for θ=0⁢°,30⁢°,45⁢°,60⁢°,90⁢° and use trigonometric identities and algebraic or geometric tricks to derive the values for trigonometric functions for other angles.

Possible duplicate of [how can we show π28=1+132+152+172+…?](http://math.stackexchange.com/questions/1454960/how-can-we-show-frac-pi28-1-frac132-frac152-frac172)

Let the required sum be S.

112+122+132+142+⋯=π26

⟹112+132+152+172+⋯+122+142+⋯=π26

⟹S+122⁢(112+122+132+142+⋯)=π26

⟹S+14⋅π26=π26

⟹S=π26-π224=π28

Hope this helps.

(a+b+c)*(1a+1b+1c)≥9.

This question was [recently asked and answered.](http://math.stackexchange.com/questions/674622/discrete-math-proofs-involving-real-numbers)

This is an answer by a different method of those given by the link of André Nicolas.

By Cauchy-Schwarz inequality we have (∑iai⁢bi)2≤(∑iai2)⁢(∑ibi2) so it suffices to take ai=a and bi=b-1/2 and we have the desired result.

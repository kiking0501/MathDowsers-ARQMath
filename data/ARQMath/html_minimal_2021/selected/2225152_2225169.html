@FlewittConnor Factorization is also an option. Consider the roots are α-α2-β and α+α2-β , so we have to show x2-2⁢α⁢x+β=(x-α-α2-β)⋅(x-α+α2-β)
To show that simplify the right side using (a-b)2=a2-2⁢a⁢b+b2

Consider f⁢(x)=x2-2⁢α⁢x+β=0

f⁢(x)=(x-α)2+β-α2=0

Then x=α±α2-β as required.

Bernoulli's Inequality implies that (1+xn)n is increasing in n for x⁢\gt-n. This implies that for x⁢\gt⁢0 (1+xn)n≤ex and for n⁢\gt⁢x⁢\gt⁢0 (1-xn)n≤e-x and so (1-xn)-n≥ex

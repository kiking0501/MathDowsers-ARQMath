5, *even*. Just see below.

2i2 mod 7 is determined by i2 mod 6, hence by i mod 6. That should suffice to sort out the remainder. Now the parity of the quotient depends only on the remainder of the sum mod 14, which you can get from knowing the sum mod 7 and mod 2.

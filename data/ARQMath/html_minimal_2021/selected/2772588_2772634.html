Let k denote the order of a⁢b modulo p. Then we have that:

1≡(a⁢b)r⁢k≡ar⁢k⁢br⁢k≡br⁢kmodp

Then we have that s∣r⁢k, but as gcd⁡(r,s)=1 we have that s∣k. Similarly we prove that r∣k and hence r⁢s∣k. As you have already proven that k∣r⁢s we conclude that k=r⁢s.

An answer for the direction "if ak=bk,|ak|=1,k>1 then the equation holds":

Since ak=bk,k>1, let X=∏k>1ak. From |ak|=1 we get |X|=1. Further, |ak-bk|=0,k>1. Then

|a1⁢X-b1⁢X|=|a1-b1|⁢|X|=|a1-b1|

which proves this direction.

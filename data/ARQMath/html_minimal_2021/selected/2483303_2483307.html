Think about the prime factorization of m and n and what being relatively prime means.

Rename: m→a, n→b and N→n

Proof: Since a|n we can write n=a⁢k. Now since b|a⁢k we have, by Euclid lemma b|k, so k=b⁢l. Thus n=a⁢b⁢l and so a⁢b|n.

Vice versa. Say a⁢b|n and since a|a⁢b we have by transitivity a|n and the same holds for b.

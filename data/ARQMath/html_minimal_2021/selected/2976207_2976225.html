The quadratic formula doesn't give that -a±a2-42≠0, but instead that -a±a2-42≠x.

you need to be very careful with your inequalities, since for example setting setting a=-2 yields that x=2 is a solution of your denominator. In particular you need not look for ≠0 but you need to look for ≠x, since the existence of a soltion to your quadratic equation would yield to a zero of the denominator. Hence if you pick a such that a2<4 you are done, since then your squareroot does not exist in ℝ (pictorially your parabola does not touch the x axis), and so the fraction is defined everywhere.

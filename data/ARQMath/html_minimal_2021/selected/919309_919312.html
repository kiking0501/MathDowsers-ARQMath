Yes you're right, that question is exactly what I needed.

This can be written as x⁢∑n=1∞n⁢xn-1 and the series is the derivative of ∑n=0∞xn=11-x

Though I have seen this many times in my lifetime (and a lot on MSE) this is still up there as a beautifully simple way to solve a rather hard problem :) +1.

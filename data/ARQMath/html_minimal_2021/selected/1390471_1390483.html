If A1A2A3…..An be a regular polygon and 1A1⁢A2=1A1⁢A3+1A1⁢A4.Then find the value of n(number of vertices in the regular polygon).

I know that sides of a regular polygon are equal but i could not relateA1⁢A3 and A1⁢A4 with the side length.Can someone assist me in solving this problem?

I'm sorry I cannot draw a diagram and explain, though that would be very neat. Let r be the radius of the circumcircle, and let 2⁢θ be the angle subtended at the center by the adjacent vertices. If a is the length of any side of the regular polygon, then a=2⁢r⁢sin⁡θ. Now θ=πn, then the given equation becomes 1sin⁡θ=1sin⁡2⁢θ+1sin⁡3⁢θ. Simplify this, you will get sin⁡4⁢θ=sin⁡3⁢θ, gives θ=π7. So n=7 is the final answer. You may fill in the gaps in the solution.

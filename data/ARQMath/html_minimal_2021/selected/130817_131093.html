Apply the double angle formula cos⁡2⁢θ=cos2⁡θ-sin2⁡θ=2⁢cos2⁡θ-1 to θ=π5 and 2⁢π5, with a=cos⁡π5 and b=2⁢π5 for convenience, recalling also that cos⁡(π±θ)=-cos⁡θ: b=cos⁡2⁢π5=2⁢cos2⁡π5-1=2⁢a2-1 -a=cos⁡4⁢π5=2⁢cos2⁡2⁢π5-1=2⁢b2-1 Next, subtracting the equations we get

2⁢(a2-b2)amp;=b+a2⁢(a+b)⁢(a-b)amp;=b+a2⁢(a-b)amp;=1a-bamp;=12. Furthermore, multiplying, we get 4⁢(a⁢b)2=(1+b)⁢(1-a)=1+(b-a)-a⁢b=1+(-12)-a⁢b giving us the quadratic equation 4⁢(a⁢b)2+(a⁢b)-12=0 8⁢(a⁢b)2+2⁢(a⁢b)-1=0 (4⁢a⁢b-1)⁢(2⁢a⁢b+1)=0 so that a⁢b=14 or -12, from which we can choose the former since we know that 0⁢&⁢l⁢t⁢a⁢&⁢l⁢t⁢b.

Series question with logarithms

I want to know how to check the divergence of following sum: ∑k=0∞1log⁡nn

I tried to use this result: limn→∞⁡1log⁡nn=1≠0 but I don't know why this result is true.

Best regards.

calculus sequences-and-series summation

First, I assume that the index of summation is n (not k), otherwise the sum diverges trivially. Otherwise, note that (for n>1) we have nn>n>log⁡n. Hence n>nn>log⁡nn. We take reciprocals to get 1n<1log⁡nn

Now you can use the comparison test to complete the problem.

I apologize for chiming in so late, but I believe there is an error in this solution. Since we want to show that F is analytic as a function defined on ℂ, we need the estimate to hold as h→0 in the complex plane (not just on the real axis). For example, the estimate |e-i⁢x⁢h-1h|≤|x| fails in general if h=i.

There are a lot of different notations. For instance, if y=f⁢(x) is derivable then the derivate can be written as f′⁢(x), y′, d⁢yd⁢x, d⁢fd⁢x, dd⁢x⁢[f⁢(x)] or even Dx⁢(f⁢(x)).

In particular the notations d⁢fd⁢x, dd⁢x⁢[f⁢(x)] and d⁢yd⁢x are called the Leibniz's notations.

If you know what "complex infinity" means, then you probably mean to ask this question in the complex projective numbers (i.e. the arithmetic of the Riemann sphere), in which case the answer is, of course, ∞. The same is true in the real projective numbers.

In the arithmetic of real numbers, of course, the answer is undefined. The same is true for the extended real numbers.

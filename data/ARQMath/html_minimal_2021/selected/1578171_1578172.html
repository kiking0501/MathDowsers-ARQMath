functions continuity

This is a consequence of the Intermediate Value Theorem. It states as follows.

Let f:[a,b]→ℝ be a continuous function and let u∈ℝ be between f⁢(a) and f⁢(b). Then, there exists a c∈[a,b] such that f⁢(c)=u.

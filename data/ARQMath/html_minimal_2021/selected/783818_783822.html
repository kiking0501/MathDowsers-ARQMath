The point (a⁢cos⁡θ,b⁢sin⁡θ) is not at angle θ from the x-axis. [This appears to be a common mistake.](http://math.stackexchange.com/q/315386/856)

Parameterization and Conversion to Polar coordinate are not linked

To parameterize hyperbola (x-α)2a2-(y-β)2b2=1⁢ and ⁢sec2⁡ϕ-tan2⁡ϕ=1

x-αa=sec⁡ϕ,y-βb=tan⁡ϕ

For Conversion to Polar coordinate always x=r⁢cos⁡θ,y=r⁢sin⁡θ

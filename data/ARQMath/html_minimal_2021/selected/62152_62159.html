The function your teacher showed you is not continuous at all irrationals (nor is it discontinuous at all rationals). It is, however, a nice example of a function which is continuous only at a single point (namely 0).

Perhaps your teacher meant Thomae's function, which is continuous at all irrational numbers and discontinuous at all rational numbers.

There is also a cheaper way for showing this : you can build a discrete partition of unity adapted to your sheaf. This is done in the book of Miranda, Algebraic curves and Riemann surfaces.

A skyscraper sheaf is flasque, hence has no cohomology: Hartshorne Chap. III, Prop.2.5, page 208.

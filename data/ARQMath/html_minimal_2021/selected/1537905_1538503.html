Suppose ∀ϵ>0,

By BCL1, we have

P⁢(lim sup⁡[|Xn|>ϵ])=0

→P(lim inf[|Xn|≤ϵ])=1

→P([lim inf|Xn|]≤ϵ)=1andP([lim sup|Xn|]≤ϵ)=1

→P([lim inf|Xn|]=0)=1andP([lim sup|Xn|]=0)=1

→P([lim|Xn|]=0)=1

→P([|limXn|]=0)=1

→P([limXn]=0)=1

'only if'

Suppose ∃ϵ0>0 s.t.

∑n=1∞P⁢(|Xn|>ϵ0)=∞

By BCL2, we have

P⁢(lim sup⁡[|Xn|>ϵ0])=1

→P⁢([lim sup⁡|Xn|]>ϵ0)=1

→P([lim sup|Xn|]≤ϵ0)=0

→P(lim inf[|Xn|≤ϵ0])=0

However since

P([limXn]=0)=1,

we have

P([lim sup|Xn|]=0)=1

→∀ϵ>0,P⁢([lim sup⁡|Xn|]<ϵ)=1

Choose ϵ=ϵ0 to get:

P(lim inf|[Xn|&lt;ϵ0])=1↯

\therefore,∀ϵ>0,∑n=1∞P⁢(|Xn|>ϵ)<∞ QED

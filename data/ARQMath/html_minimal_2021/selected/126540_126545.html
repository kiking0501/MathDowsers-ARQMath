Use the Chu-Vandermonde identity, as recommended by t.b.: (m+nr)=∑k=0r(mk)⁢(nr-k),m,n,r∈ℕ0, and set m=n, r=n and use (nn-k)=(nk) to get: ∑k=0n(nk)2=(2⁢nn)

The next term is an=1+log⁡(cos⁡(1))n+log⁡(cos⁡(1))⁢[log⁡(cos⁡(1))-2⁢tan⁡(1)]2⁢n2+⋯ and the next after that is even more complicated.

Note that an<1, hence

an=cos⁡(an)n>cos⁡(1)n

From this, we may also conclude that

an=cos⁡(an)n<cos⁡(cos⁡(1)n)n

But,

limn→∞⁡n⁢(1-cos⁡(1)n)=-ln⁡(cos⁡(1))

limn→∞⁡n⁢(1-cos⁡(cos⁡(1)n)n)=-ln⁡(cos⁡(1)) Both of which may be derived by rewriting the limits as

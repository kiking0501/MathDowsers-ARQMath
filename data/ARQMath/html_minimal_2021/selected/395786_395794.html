Proving an inequality: |1-ei⁢θ|≤|θ|

|1-ei⁢θ|≤|θ|

Draw the unit circle and the two points 1 and ei⁢θ. Draw the line segment between these points. Compare the length of this segment with the arc joining the two points.

Hint: ei⁢θ-1=∫0θiei⁢xdx,|iei⁢x|\leqslant1(x∈ℝ).

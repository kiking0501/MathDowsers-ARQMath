How to prove ⌊⌊ab⌋c⌋=⌊ab×c⌋

If a,b,c are positive integers, how to prove ⌊⌊ab⌋c⌋=⌊ab×c⌋

integers

⌊⌊ab⌋c⌋ is the greatest integer t with t≤⌊ab⌋c, that is c⁢t≤⌊ab⌋. If t is an integer, so is c⁢t and c⁢t≤⌊ab⌋ iff c⁢t≤ab, that is iff b⁢c⁢t≤a.

So, ⌊⌊ab⌋c⌋ is the greatest integer t with b⁢c⁢t≤a, that is ⌊⌊ab⌋c⌋ is ⌊ab⁢c⌋.

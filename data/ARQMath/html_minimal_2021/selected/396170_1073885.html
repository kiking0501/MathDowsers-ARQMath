Let I⁢(a)=∫0∞ln⁡(1+a⁢t)1+t2⁢𝑑t. Then I⁢(0)=0 and \begin{eqnarray} I'(a)&=&\int_0^\infty\frac{t}{(1+at)(1+t^2)}dt\\ &=&\frac{1}{1+a^2}\int_0^\infty\left(\frac{a+t}{1+t^2}-\frac{a}{1+at}\right)dt\\ &=&\frac{1}{2(1+a^2)}(2a\arctan t-2\ln(1+at)+\ln(1+t^2))\bigg|_{0}^\infty\\ &=&\frac{a\pi-2\ln a}{2(1+a^2)}. \end{eqnarray} So we have I⁢(1)=∫01a⁢π-2⁢ln⁡a2⁢(1+a2)⁢𝑑a=π4⁢ln⁡2+G where wu use ∫01ln⁡a1+a2⁢𝑑a=-G.

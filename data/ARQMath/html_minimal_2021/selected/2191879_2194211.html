The following is more straightforward that invoking Fenchel Rockafeller:

Let B¯ be the closed unit ball.

Let M=supx∈B¯∩L⁡|f⁢(x)|. Define ϕ⁢(x)=f⁢(x) for x∈L and use the Hahn Banach theorem extend ϕ to all of E in such a way that ∥ϕ∥=M. Note that g=f-ϕ∈L⊥. Then ∥f-g∥=∥ϕ∥=M, and hence infg∈L⊥⁡∥f-g∥≤supx∈B¯∩L⁡|f⁢(x)|.

For the other direction, note that if g∈L⊥,x∈B¯∩L then ∥f-g∥≥|f⁢(x)-g⁢(x)|=|f⁢(x)|. Taking the sup of the right hand side gives ∥f-g∥≥M, and hence infg∈L⊥⁡∥f-g∥≥M=supx∈B¯∩L⁡|f⁢(x)|.

Conditional convergence of a power series on the z∈ℂ for which |z|=ρ

Consider the following complex power series ∑n≥1znn  z∈ℂ

It surely converges conditionally for z=-1 (for alternating series test) and for z=1 it diverges (it is the harmonic series).

My question is: how can one show that the power series converges conditionally for any z∈ℂ such that |z|=1 (except for z=1)?

The complex version of [Abel's test](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abel's_test) will work for this purpose.

We can use the Dirichlet's test to prove the desired result: with an=1n and bn=ei⁢n⁢θ we verify that

  • (an) is decreasing to 0
  • ∑n=1Nbn is bounded

so the series ∑an⁢bn is convergent.

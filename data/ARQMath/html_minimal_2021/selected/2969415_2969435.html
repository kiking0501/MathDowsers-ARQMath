You will need to assume that αi≥0 for all i.

It will help you to know Young's inequality: if a,b≥0, p,q>1, and 1p+1q=1, then a⁢b≤app+bqq.

With p=1+1n, Young's inequality gives you (α1⁢⋯⁢αn+1)1n+1amp;=(α1⋯αn)1n+1αn+11n+1amp;≤nn+1(α1⋯αn)1n+1n+1αn+1.

You can apply the induction step if you know that (α1⁢⋯⁢αn)1n≤a1+⋯+ann.

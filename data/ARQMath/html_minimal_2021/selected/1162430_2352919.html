(Late answer, but I just flagged a recent question as a duplicate of this one, and thought I'd leave yet another way to solve it here.)

The given inequality (∑i=1nai)⁢(∑i=1n1ai)≥n2 is equivalent to ∑i=1nain≥n∑i=1n1ai.

The latter is the AM≥HM inequality between the arithmetic and harmonic means of n positive numbers, which holds true by the generalized mean inequality, with equality iff all ai are equal.

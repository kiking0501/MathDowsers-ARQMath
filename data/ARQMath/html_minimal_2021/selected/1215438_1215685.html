Sorry, I oversimplified things! See my answer below.

Let p1,…,pk be the distinct primes dividing r or s, and let r=p1r1⁢⋯⁢pkrk, s=p1s1⁢⋯⁢pksk (where some of the ri, si may be 0). So lcm⁢(r,s)=p1m1⁢⋯⁢pkmk, where mi=max⁡(ri,si).

Reorder the primes if necessary so that ri≥si for 1≤i≤j and si>ri for i<j≤k. Then ha has order p1r1⁢⋯⁢pjrj (where a=pj+1rj+1⁢⋯⁢pkrk) and similarly some power kb of k has order pj+1sj+1⁢⋯⁢pksk, so ha and kb have coprime orders, and their product ha⁢kb has the required order lcm⁢(r,s).

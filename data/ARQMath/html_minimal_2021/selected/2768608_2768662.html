In his answer, marty cohen gave nice upper and lower bounds for the partial sums in your specific problem.

If we make the problem more general (k>1), Sn(k)=∑1nk=Hn(1k) where appear the generalized harmonic numbers.

Using their asymptotics Sn(k)=kk-1⁢n(1-1k)+ζ⁢(1k)+12⁢n1k+O⁢(1n(1+1k)) where ζ⁢(1k) is a negative number which goes asymptotically to -12.

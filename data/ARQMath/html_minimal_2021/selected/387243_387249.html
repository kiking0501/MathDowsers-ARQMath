Hint: take the transposes of A⁢AT and AT⁢A. Use what you know about the transpose to show (A⁢AT)T=A⁢AT, and (AT⁢A)T=AT⁢A

In particular, if you are still struggling:

(A⁢AT)T=(AT)T⁢AT=A⁢AT

+1: Just to make sure that OP sees the light: the rule in use here is that for all matrices X,Y (X⁢Y)T=YT⁢XT. Plug in X=A, Y=AT (and the other way around).

There are 3 basic(1,2 and 3) formula regarding logarithm:

1.l⁢o⁢ga⁢m+l⁢o⁢ga⁢n=l⁢o⁢ga⁢(m⁢n)

2.l⁢o⁢ga⁢m-l⁢o⁢ga⁢n=l⁢o⁢ga⁢(mn)

3.l⁢o⁢ga⁢mn=n×l⁢o⁢ga⁢(m)

4.l⁢o⁢ga⁢a=1

5.l⁢o⁢ga⁢b×l⁢o⁢gn⁢a=logn⁡b

6.l⁢o⁢ga⁢ml⁢o⁢ga⁢n=l⁢o⁢gn⁢m

and the basic which useful for solving log equation

loga⁡b=n⟺an=b

These can solve many problems regarding logarithm.

HINT: The information given in the problem allows you to calculate the number of edges in T in terms of the numbers n1,n2,…,nm. Since T is a tree, the number of edges tells you the number of vertices. You know (in terms of n1,n2,…,nm) how many of those vertices have children, so you can calculate the number that have no children.

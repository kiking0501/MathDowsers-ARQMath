We have to determine the probability that a random word of length 4 over the alphabet {𝚊,…,𝚣,𝙰,…,𝚉,𝟶,…⁢𝟿} satisfies certain conditions. There are 624=14 776 336 equiprobable words.

All admissible words consist of four different symbols. Two of the symbols have to be alphabetical and two of them have to be numerical. I assume that pairs of type {𝚊,𝙰} are forbidden. We then can choose two letters from the alphabet in (262)=325 ways and then realize each of these choices as symbol pair in 4 ways: {𝚊,𝚋},{𝚊,𝙱},(x-a)⁢(x-b)2,{𝙰,𝙱}. The numerical pair can be chosen in (102)=45 ways. When the four symbols have been chosen we can arrange them in 4!=24 ways. It follows that there are 325⋅4⋅45⋅24=1 404 000 admissible words.

The probability p in question then comes to p=1 404 00014 776 336≐0.0950.

modules direct-sum projective-module free-modules

it is well known that one characterization of a projective module is that it is a direct summand of a free module.

to see this, let P be projective and choose a generating set S={mα}α∈A

let F be the free module on a set X isomorphic to S and look at the surjective map: p:F→P defined by p⁢(xα)=mα

by the defining property of a projective module there is a map q:P→F (a lift of the identity map P→P) which satisfies q∘p=i⁢dP

you now have: F=k⁢e⁢r⁢(p)⊕i⁢m⁢(q)

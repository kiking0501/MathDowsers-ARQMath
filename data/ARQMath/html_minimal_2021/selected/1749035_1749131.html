Rudin *Real and Complex Analysis* 15.5: "Suppose 0≤un<1. Then ∏n=1∞(1-un)>0 if and only if ∑n=1∞un<∞."

We can write

an=2-12⁢3-13⁢⋯⁢n+1-1n+1=12⁢(2+1)⁢23⁢(3+1)⁢⋯⁢nn+1⁢(n+1+1)

For every positive integer k, k⁢(k+1)>k⋅k=k; therefore

an<12⁢23⁢⋯⁢nn+1=1n+1

Since lim⁡1n+1=0 and an is positive, by the squeeze theorem we must have lim⁡an=0.

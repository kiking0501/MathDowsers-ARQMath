In your calculation of the covariance you have assumed that they are independent. Then it is no surprise that the covariance is zero.
Covariance is 𝖢𝗈𝗏(X,Y)=∑(x,y)∈{-1,0,1}2(x-𝖤(X))(y-𝖤(Y))𝖯(X=x,Y=y)=∑(x,y)∈{-1,0,1}2xy𝖯(X=x,Y=y) So... Without knowing the joint pmf you *cannot* calculate the covariance.
Note also that the formula for covarinance you have used is for *independent uniform distributions*, which is not compatible with the marginal distributions you had been given.

We have the highlighted inequality because for f∈E* and x∈E we have |f⁢(x)|≤∥f∥⁢∥x∥, and you're taking the supremum over ∥f∥≤1. This can be seen by observing that for x∈E nonzero, x/∥x∥ has norm 1, so |f⁢(x∥x∥)|≤supx∈E,||x||≤1⁡|f⁢(x)|=∥f∥. Multiplying by ∥x∥ yields |f⁢(x)|≤∥f∥⁢∥x∥.

Let v⁢(x)=y⁢(x)+x2/2. Then v′⁢(x)=y′⁢(x)+x, so adding x2/2 to both sides of your DE gives v=12⁢v′⁣2. I'm sure you can separate v and x here.

Caveat: If you separate the variables it's easy to forget the special solution v=0 (or y=-x2/2).

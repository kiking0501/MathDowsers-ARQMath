You are proceeding correctly, but note the exponential distribution is only non-zero for positive arguments so the limits of integration will be from 0 to a. Also, the second factor is missing a 2 in the exponent 2⁢λ⁢e-2⁢λ⁢y. You should end up with a linear combination of the original exponentials.

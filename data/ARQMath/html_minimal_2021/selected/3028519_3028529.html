You are right; the paragraph starting with “Finally” in the answer you refer to is wrong.

I think your error may be in the direction of implication

Your link says ∀ϵ>0,∃δ>0⁢ s.t. ⁢|x-a|<δ⟹|f⁢(x)-f⁢(a)|<ϵ but you seem to have interpreted it as ∀ϵ>0,∃δ>0⁢ s.t. ⁢|f⁢(x)-f⁢(a)|<ϵ⟹|x-a|<δ

The problem here is that the answerer there maintains that the “reverse condition” is satisfied by every function.

Your attempt would probably carry through if you consider the for these purposes equivalent function f⁢(x)=exe-x.

The graph γ of log is concave, hence γ stays below the tangent to γ at (e,log⁡e): log⁡x≤log⁡e+1e⁢(x-e)  (x>0). This simplifies to log⁡x≤xe, and exponentiation gives xe≤ex  (x>0). The case x=0 is obvious.

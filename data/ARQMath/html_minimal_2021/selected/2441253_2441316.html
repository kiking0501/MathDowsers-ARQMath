You have the wrong approach. Use the polar representation of complex numbers.

From your equation (b), you have (a2-b2)2-4⁢a2⁢b2=0 then (a2-2⁢a⁢b-b2)⁢(a2+2⁢a⁢b-b2)=0.

From equation (c), you have a⁢b⁢(b2-a2)=4.

Case 1: a2-2⁢a⁢b-b2=0 and a⁢b⁢(b2-a2)=4. You get (a⁢b)2=2, then a⁢b=2 or a⁢b=-2.

For a⁢b=2, b2-a2=2⁢2. Then 2a2-a2=2⁢2, you get a4+2⁢2⁢a2-2=0, then a2=-2+2, so b2=2+2.

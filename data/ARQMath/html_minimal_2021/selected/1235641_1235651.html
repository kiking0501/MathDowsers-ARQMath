1,2,3,4,5,…,n/1!

1,3,6,10,15,21,28,…,n⁢(n+1)/2!

etc.

You may have heard of Pascal's Triangle. Each of these sequences fills a sloping line of the triangle.
It can be explaind by induction. For example, n⁢(n+1)⁢(n+2)⁢(n+3)24+(n+1)⁢(n+2)⁢(n+3)6=(n+1)⁢(n+2)⁢(n+3)⁢(n+4)24 which shows that adding the fourth series gives the fifth seres.

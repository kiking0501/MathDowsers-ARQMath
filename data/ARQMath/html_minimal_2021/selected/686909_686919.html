Yes, the primal optimal value is infx⁡supλ≥0⁡L⁢(x,λ) and the dual optimal value is supλ≥0⁡infx⁡L⁢(x,λ). A primal and dual optimal pair of variables gives you a saddle point of the Lagrangian. This is discussed on p. 238 (section 5.4.1) of Boyd and Vandenberghe, and is also discussed in other convex optimization books such as Borwein and Lewis (p. 88, section 4.3).

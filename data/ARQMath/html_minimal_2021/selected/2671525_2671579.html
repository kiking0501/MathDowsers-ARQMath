Raise both sides to k⁢(k+1) we get:

kk+1>(k+1)k

To prove: k>(1+1k)k

Assume true for k=n; and now consider the case k=n+1

Then n+1>(1+1n)n+1

multiplying both sides with (1+1n) we get

(n+1)⁢(1+1n)=n+1+1+1n>(1+1n)n+1+1+1n>(1+1n+1)n+1+1+1n

⟹n+1>(1+1n+1)n+1

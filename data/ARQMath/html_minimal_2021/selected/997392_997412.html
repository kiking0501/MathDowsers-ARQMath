The Dirichlet beta function may satisfy you : β⁢(x):=∑n=0∞(-1)n(2⁢n+1)x with the table of values : \begin{array} {c|c} n&\beta(n)\\ \hline 1&\frac {\pi}4\\ 2&K\\ 3&\frac {\pi^3}{32}\\ 4&\beta(4)\\ 5&\frac {5\,\pi^5}{1536}\\ \end{array} with K the Catalan constant.

Here the n even cases are the difficult ones as opposed to the ζ odd cases !

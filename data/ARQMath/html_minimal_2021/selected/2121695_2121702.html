The Taylor series is a series of functions of the form:

f⁢(x)=∑n=0∞an⁢(x-a)n, where an=f(n)⁢(a)n!. This series is called the Taylor series of f⁢(x) around the point x=a. In the particular case of a=0 the series is called the Maclaurin series.

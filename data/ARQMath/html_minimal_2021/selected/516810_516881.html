I'll use yT for the OP's y⊤.

Observe that xT⁢A⁢x˙, being a scalar quantity, is automatically symmetric: (xT⁢A⁢x˙)T=xT⁢A⁢x˙, and (x˙T⁢A⁢x)T=x˙T⁢A⁢x as well. Now (xT⁢A⁢x˙)T=x˙T⁢AT⁢x, so we obtain xT⁢A⁢x˙=x˙T⁢AT⁢x. Thus

xT⁢A⁢x˙+x˙T⁢A⁢x=x˙T⁢AT⁢x+x˙T⁢A⁢x=x˙T⁢(AT+A)⁢x.

You could replace j by i and simplify.

Another variation which might be helpful.

\begin{align*} \sum_{i=1}^n (i + 1) - \sum_{j=1}^n j&=\sum_{i=1}^n (i + 1) - \sum_{i=1}^n i\\ &=\sum_{i=1}^n (i + 1-i)\\ &=\sum_{i=1}^n 1\\ &=n \end{align*}

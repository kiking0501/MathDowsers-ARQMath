Question regarding algebraicity of two elements whose sum and product are algebraic.

Let α,β∈ℂ and suppose α+β and α⁢β are algebraic over ℚ. Prove α,β are algebraic over ℚ.

Your question is equivalent to asking to prove that the roots of a quadratic polynomial with algebraic coefficients are themselves algebraic, which is well-known, and true for all polynomials, regardless of their order.

Observe that α2-(α+β)⁢α+α⁢β=0. Hence, α is algebraic over ℚ⁢(α+β,α⁢β). But ℚ⁢(α+β,α⁢β) is algebraic over ℚ. Hence, α is algebraic over ℚ.

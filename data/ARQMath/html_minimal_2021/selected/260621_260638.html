hints: 1) Integration by parts 2) Derivative of t⁢a⁢n-1⁢(x).

Putting x=a⁢tan⁡t,d⁢x=a⁢sec2⁡t⁢d⁢t and x=0⟹t=0,x=∞⟹t=π2

∫0+∞ln⁡xa2+x2⁢𝑑x=1a⁢∫0π2ln⁡(a⁢tan⁡t)⁢𝑑t

∫0π2ln⁡(a⁢tan⁡t)⁢𝑑t=ln⁡a⁢∫0π2𝑑t+∫0π2ln⁡tan⁡t⁢d⁢t

Now,

∫0π2ln⁡tan⁡t⁢d⁢t =∫0π2ln⁡tan⁡(π2+0-t)⁢d⁢t

(as ∫abf⁢(a+b-x)⁢𝑑x=∫baf⁢(t)⁢(-d⁢t) (putting a+b-x=t)

∫abf⁢(a+b-x)⁢𝑑x=∫abf⁢(t)⁢𝑑t=∫abf⁢(x)⁢𝑑x)

=∫0π2ln⁡cot⁡t⁢d⁢t =-∫0π2ln⁡tan⁡t⁢d⁢t⟹∫0π2ln⁡tan⁡t⁢d⁢t=0

and

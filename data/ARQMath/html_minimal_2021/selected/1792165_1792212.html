Sign of third order derivative

We have a concave function f⁢(x,y), i.e. the Hessian matrix has non-positive elements. Can we show ∂3⁡f∂⁡x⁢∂⁡y2≤0?

partial-derivative

The answer is no. Take f⁢(x,y)=-(x-y)4 for example. We have ∂3⁡f∂⁡x⁢∂⁡y2=-24⁢(x-y) which could be positive or negative depending on values of x and y.

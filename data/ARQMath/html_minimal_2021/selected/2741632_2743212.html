limn→∞⁡(∑r=0n2r52r+1) =limn→∞⁡∑r=0n(2r52r+1⋅52r-152r-1) =limn→∞⁡∑r=0n(2r⁢((52r+1)-2)52r+1-1) =limn→∞⁡∑r=0n(2r52r-1-2r+152r+1-1) =15-1=14

Note: In fact using this method it can be proved that for any natural number a (except of course $a=1$) limn→∞⁡(∑r=0n2ra2r+1)=1a-1

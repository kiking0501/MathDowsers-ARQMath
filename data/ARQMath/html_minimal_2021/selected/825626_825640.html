Integral and continuous function

Let f:[0,1]→ℝ be a continuous function. Supose that ∫01xn⁢f⁢(x)⁢𝑑x=0

∀n=0,1,2,…. Proof that f⁢(x)=0.

real-analysis

Hint

These are the steps:

  • Prove that for any polynomial P: ∫01P⁢(x)⁢f⁢(x)⁢𝑑x=0
  • Use the Stone–Weierstrass theorem
  • and finally use the fact that if (since f is continuous) ∫01f2⁢(x)⁢𝑑x=0 then f is the zero function.

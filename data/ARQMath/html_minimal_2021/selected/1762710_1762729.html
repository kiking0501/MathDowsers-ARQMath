Just to complete Ron Gordon's excellent answer, notice that: I⁢(a,b)=∫0+∞d⁢xex⁢log⁡a+b=1log⁡a⁢∫0+∞d⁢zez+b=1log⁡a⁢∫1+∞d⁢tt⁢(t+b) and: ∫1+∞d⁢tt⁢(t+b)=∫01d⁢u1+b⁢u=1b⁢∫0bd⁢v1+v=log⁡(1+b)b through the substitutions x=zlog⁡a, z=log⁡t, t=1u and u=vb. That leads to: ∫0+∞∑k≥1d⁢x(ks+1)x+ks=∑k≥1log⁡(1+ks)ks⁢log⁡(1+ks)=ζ⁢(s) as wanted.

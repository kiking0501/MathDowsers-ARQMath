Since adding p modulo k is about as easy as any operation can be, perhaps the generic question is about exponentiating pn modulo k, a topic dealt with in previous questions.

As you already know (a+b)mod n = ((a mod n) + (b mod n)) mod n . So I guess addition here is not a problem.

The real question seems to be on pn mod k where n is large. For that, have a look at Modular Exponentiation on wikipedia.

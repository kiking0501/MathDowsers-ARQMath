Delta cannot involve x. Remember that δ defines the range of x-values being considered, so it doesn't make sense for it to have x in it.

Note that

|xx2+1|<1,∀x∈ℝ. So, taking ϵ=δ you have

|x+1|<δ⇒|x2+xx2+1|=|x⁢(x+1)x2+1|=|x+1|⁢|xx2+1|<|x+1|<δ=ϵ.

First step: denote α=x⁢t. You need to prove that |e-i⁢α-1|≤|α| for α≠0
See also: [Prove |ei⁢θ-1|≤|θ|](http://math.stackexchange.com/questions/584498/prove-ei-theta-1-leq-theta)

HINT

\begin{align} \exp(-ixt) - 1 &= \cos(xt) - i \sin(xt) - 1\\ & = -\left(2 \sin^2(xt/2) + 2i \cos(xt/2) \sin(xt/2)\right)\\ &= -2i \sin(xt/2) \left(\cos(xt/2) - i \sin(xt/2)\right)\\ & = -2i \sin(xt/2) \exp(-ixt/2) \end{align} Hence, |exp⁡(-i⁢x⁢t)-1|=|-2⁢i⁢sin⁡(x⁢t/2)⁢exp⁡(-i⁢x⁢t/2)|=2⁢|sin⁡(x⁢t/2)|

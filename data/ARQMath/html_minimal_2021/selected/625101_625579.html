Here's a couple other trigonometric approaches:

cos⁡(2⁢π5)+cos⁡(4⁢π5)+cos⁡(6⁢π5)+cos⁡(8⁢π5)

=[cos⁡(2⁢π5)+cos⁡(8⁢π5)]+[cos⁡(4⁢π5)+cos⁡(6⁢π5)]

=(2⋅cos⁡[8⁢π5+2⁢π52]⋅cos⁡[8⁢π5-2⁢π52])+(2⋅cos⁡[6⁢π5+4⁢π52]⋅cos⁡[6⁢π5-4⁢π52])

=(2⋅cos⁡π⋅cos⁡3⁢π5)+(2⋅cos⁡π⋅cos⁡π5)=-2⋅(cos⁡3⁢π5+cos⁡π5)

=-2⋅(2⋅cos⁡[3⁢π5+π52]⋅cos⁡[3⁢π5-π52])=-4⋅cos⁡2⁢π5⋅cos⁡π5

=-4⋅(2⁢cos2⁡π5- 1)⋅cos⁡π5= 4⁢cos⁡π5- 8⁢cos3⁡π5,

to which we shall return. We also have

cos⁡(2⁢π5)+cos⁡(4⁢π5)+cos⁡(6⁢π5)+cos⁡(8⁢π5)

=cos⁡(2⁢π5)+cos⁡(4⁢π5)+cos⁡(-4⁢π5)+cos⁡(-2⁢π5)

= 2⋅[cos⁡(2⁢π5)+cos⁡(4⁢π5)]= 2⋅[cos⁡(2⁢π5)+(2⁢cos2⁡(2⁢π5)- 1)]

= 4⁢cos2⁡(2⁢π5)+ 2⁢cos⁡(2⁢π5)- 2.

A bit unfortunately, we do not get any obvious simplification; instead, we need to bring in the values for cos⁡(π5)  and cos⁡(2⁢π5), which are known exactly. [One discussion can be found here . ] We thus find

4⁢cos⁡π5- 8⁢cos3⁡π5= 4⁢(1+54)- 8⁢(14+54)3

=(1+5)- 8⁢(1+ 3⁢5+ 15+ 5⁢564)=(1+5)-(2+5)=-1

or

4⁢cos2⁡(2⁢π5)+ 2⁢cos⁡(2⁢π5)- 2= 4⁢(5- 14)2+ 2⁢(5- 14)- 2

=(5- 2⁢5+ 14)+(5- 12)- 2=32-12- 2=-1.

Naturally, this is related to the methods discussed by the other solvers through geometry in the complex plane.

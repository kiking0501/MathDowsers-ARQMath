How can I find the coefficient of x23 in (x3+x4+x5+x6)4?

How can I find the coefficient of x23 with polynomial expansion?

combinatorics generating-functions

The highest term is x24, obtained by using x6 in each factor. To get a term in x23, three of the four factors must be x6 and one x5. There are 4 choices of which of the four factors is x5. Thus the coefficient is 4.

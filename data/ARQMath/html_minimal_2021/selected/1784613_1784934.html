Note that (n+rr)=(n+rn) is the number of subsets of {1,2,…,n+r} of size n. On the other hand, for i=0,1,2,…,r, (n+i-1i)=(n+i-1n-1) is the number of subsets of {1,2,…,n+r} of size n whose largest element is n+i.

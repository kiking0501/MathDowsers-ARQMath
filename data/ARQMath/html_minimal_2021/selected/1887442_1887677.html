Can you find the general solution of d4⁢yd⁢x4=y?

Suppose you were looking for solutions to dn⁢yd⁢xn=y. Obvious solutions might be:

  • y0=1+xnn!+x2⁢n(2⁢n)!+x3⁢n(3⁢n)!+⋯
  • y1=x+xn+1(n+1)!+x2⁢n+1(2⁢n+1)!+x3⁢n+1(3⁢n+1)!+⋯
  • y2=x22!+xn+2(n+2)!+x2⁢n+2(2⁢n+2)!+x3⁢n+2(3⁢n+2)!+⋯
  • ⋯
  • yn-1=xn-1(n-1)!+x2⁢n-1(2⁢n-1)!+x3⁢n-1(3⁢n-1)!+x4⁢n-1(4⁢n-1)!+⋯

and in fact these yj=∑k=0∞xk⁢n+j(k⁢n+j)! for 0≤j⁢\lt⁢n and linear combinations of these yjs are the only solutions to dn⁢yd⁢xn=y (including the constant 0).

For example with n=4, you have solutions such as

  • ex=y0+y1+y2+y3
  • e-x=y0-y1+y2-y3
  • sin⁡(x)=y1-y3
  • cos⁡(x)=y0-y2
  • sinh⁡(x)=y1+y3
  • cosh⁡(x)=y0+y2

but you could also have something like 7⁢y1-2⁢y2 as a solution

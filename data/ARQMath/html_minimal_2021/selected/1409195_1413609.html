A solution by Sumit Ray

a⁢c=-b⁢d

ab=-dc=k

a=b⁢k⁢ and ⁢d=-c⁢k

a2+b2=1⟹b2=1k2+1⟹c2=1k2+1

Thus b2-c2=0

Now \begin{align*}ab+cd &= b^2\cdot k-c^2\cdot k\\&= k(b^2-c^2) = 0\end{align*}

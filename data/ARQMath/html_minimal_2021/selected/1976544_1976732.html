Conditional distribution of subvector of a Dirichlet random variable

Suppose 𝐗=(X1,⋯,XK) follows a Dirichlet distribution with parameters (α1,⋯,αK). Partition 𝐗=(𝐗(1),𝐗(2)) for subvectors 𝐗(1),𝐗(2). What is the distribution of 𝐗(1)|𝐗(2)?

By the aggregation property, we know that (X1,X2,X0=1-X1-X2=∑i=3kXi)∼Dir(α1,α2,α0=∑i=3kαi)

And therefore their joint pdf is fX1,X2⁢(x1,x2)=Γ⁢(α1+α2+α0)Γ⁢(α1)⁢Γ⁢(α2)⁢Γ⁢(α0)⁢x1α1-1⁢x2α2-1⁢(1-x1-x2)α0-1

where x1,x2∈(0,1),x1+x2≤1. Similarly, the marginal pdf of X2 is fX2⁢(x2)=Γ⁢(α2+α1+α0)Γ⁢(α2)⁢Γ⁢(α1+α0)⁢x2α2-1⁢(1-x2)α1+α0-1,x2∈(0,1)

i.e. Beta⁢(α2,α1+α0). Therefore the conditional pdf of X1|X2=x2 is fX1|X2=x2⁢(x1|x2)=fX1,X2⁢(x1,x2)fX2⁢(x2)=Γ⁢(α1+α0)Γ⁢(α1)⁢Γ⁢(α0)⁢(x11-x2)α1-1⁢(1-x11-x2)α0-1⁢11-x2

where x1∈(0,1-x2). So X1|X2=x2 has a scaled Beta distribution, i.e. 11-x2⁢X1|X2=x2∼Beta⁢(α1,α0)

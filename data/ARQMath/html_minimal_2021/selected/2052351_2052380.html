I think this problem was given at Putnam, 1966.

Since (an)n is eventually decreasing to 0+ then (1/an)n is a strictly increasing and divergent sequence, and we can use Stolz-Cesaro theorem: limn→∞⁡n⁢an=limn→∞⁡n1/an=SClimn→∞⁡(n+1)-n1/an+1-1/an=limn→∞⁡11an⁢(1-an)-1an=limn→∞⁡(1-an)=1.

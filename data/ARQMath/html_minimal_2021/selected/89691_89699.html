This can be solved with a smart use of Fubini's theorem:

∫a+∞h⁢(x)⁢𝑑xamp;=∫a+∞∫x-ax+af(t)dtdxamp;=∫a+∞∫0+∞1[x-a,x+a](t)f(t)dtdxamp;=∫a+∞∫0+∞1[t-a,t+a](x)f(t)dtdxamp;=∫0+∞f(t)∫a+∞1[t-a,t+a](x)dxdt.

Now, you can check that ∫a+∞1[t-a,t+a]⁢(x)⁢𝑑x=min⁡{t,2⁢a}. Hence,

∫a+∞h⁢(x)⁢𝑑xamp;=∫0+∞f(t)min{t,2a}dtamp;≤2a∫0+∞f(t)dt.

∫aXh⁢(x)⁢𝑑xamp;=∫aXF(x+a)-F(x-a)dxamp;=∫2⁢aX+aF(x)dx-∫0X-aF(x)dxamp;=∫X-aX+aF(x)dx-∫02⁢aF(x)dx.

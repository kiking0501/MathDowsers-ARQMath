Have you tried to check if it is divisible by 2 ? As a hint, notice that 33n and 33n+1 are two consecutive integers

define w=33n

Your number is then w⁢(w+1)+3⁢w-1=w2+w+3⁢w-1=w2+4⁢w-1

Now, w is odd, so w2 is odd, so w2+4⁢w-1 is even. Also, w≥3, so w2+4⁢w-1 is bigger than 4 and even, therefore not prime.

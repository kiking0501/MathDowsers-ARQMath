First step: denote α=x⁢t. You need to prove that |e-i⁢α-1|≤|α| for α≠0
See also: [Prove |ei⁢θ-1|≤|θ|](http://math.stackexchange.com/questions/584498/prove-ei-theta-1-leq-theta)

As explained by @Dennis Gulko in a comment, the task is to prove that |e-i⁢s-1|⁢\leqslant⁢|s| for every real number s. But the function u:ℝ→ℂ, t↦e-i⁢t, has derivative u′⁢(t)=-ie-i⁢t, whose modulus is (at most) 1 everywhere, hence |u⁢(s)-u⁢(0)|⁢\leqslant⁢|s-0|⋅sup⁡{|u′⁢(t)|∣0⁢\leqslant⁢t⁢\leqslant⁢s}=|s|.

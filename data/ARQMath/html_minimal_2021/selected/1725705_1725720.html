Just to add to the previous answer: a clearer argument (I think) is to proceed in two steps. You certainly have max∥x∥=1⁡∥A⁢x∥=max∥x∥=1⁡∥A⁢x∥∥x∥≤maxx≠0⁡∥A⁢x∥∥x∥.

For the reverse inequality, fix x≠0. Then ∥A⁢x∥∥x∥=∥A⁢x∥x∥∥≤max∥y∥=1⁡∥A⁢y∥, and so taking the maximum over nonzero x gives the result.

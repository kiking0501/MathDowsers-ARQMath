Notation for the domain of f⁢(𝐱,𝐲)?

I have two vectors, 𝐱∈ℝn and 𝐲∈ℝm and the real-valued function f⁢(𝐱,𝐲)=f⁢(x1,…,xn,y1,…⁢ym) Is the domain ℝn×ℝm=ℝn+m or ℝn×ℝm=ℝn⁢m?

The set ℝN consists of N-tuples of real numbers. That is, an element in ℝN is built from N real numbers.

Clearly, your function has N=n+m and not n⁢m real numbers as input, hence it cannot be ℝn⁢m, but it is ℝn×ℝm=ℝn+m.

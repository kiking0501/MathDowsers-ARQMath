Learning how to prove things is a skill you learn yourself through practice...there is no book that will make you create your own proofs of things. This is not just a "topic of maths" that can be learned in a few hours but the basis of all mathematical thought!
This skill takes a little time to cultivate, but you should be able to make rapid progress if you are determined! You can probably get helpful feedback on your proof-work here, too.

It is very easy to check that X⁢Y and Y⁢X have the same nonzero eigenvalues. Just apply Y to the identity X⁢Y⁢v=λ⁢v and note Y⁢v≠0 if λ⁢v≠0.

But if you mean λ to be the set of eigenvalues counted with multiplicities, then what you are asking is equivalent to χX⁢Y⁢(t)=tm-n⁢χY⁢X⁢(t) where χA⁢(t)=det⁡(t⁢I-A) is the charateristic polynomial.

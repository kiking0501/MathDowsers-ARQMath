I'll start with the inductive step

3⋅52⁢(n+1)+1+23⁢(n+1)+1

3⋅52⁢n+1+2+23⁢n+1+3

3⋅25⋅52⁢(n+1)+8⋅23⁢n+1

We know that 3⋅52⁢n+1+23⁢n+1=17⁢k

Therefore 3⋅52⁢n+1=17⁢k-23⁢n+1

Plug this in: 25⋅(17⁢k-23⁢n+1)+8⋅23⁢n+1

425k⋅-25⋅23⁢n+1+8⋅23⁢n+1

425k⋅-17⋅23⁢n+1

17⁢(25-23⁢n+1)

Prove 8⁢cos⁡(x)⁢cos⁡(2⁢x)⁢cos⁡(3⁢x)-1=sin⁡(7⁢x)sin⁡(x)

How do you prove that 8⁢cos⁡(x)⁢cos⁡(2⁢x)⁢cos⁡(3⁢x)-1=sin⁡(7⁢x)sin⁡(x)?

Multiply both sides by sin⁡x, then

2⁢cos⁡x⁢sin⁡x=sin⁡2⁢x

2⁢cos⁡2⁢x⁢sin⁡2⁢x=sin⁡4⁢x

2⁢sin⁡4⁢x⁢cos⁡3⁢x=sin⁡7⁢x+sin⁡x

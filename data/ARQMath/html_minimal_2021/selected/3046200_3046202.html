Maximum number of distinct prime factors of numbers below 264

What is the maximum number of distinct prime factors of numbers below 264? I'm interested in the exact count, not just an estimate.

In other words, what is the largest ω⁢(n), where n<264?

The number with the most distinct prime factors will have the form

2⋅3⋅5⋅7⁢⋯

So all you need is to multiply all primes until you reach 264.

You can get this from two simple equivalences:

  1. P∨Q⇔¬⁢P→Q

  2. (P∧Q)→R⇔P→(Q→R)

Here (P∧¬⁢Q)→R is equivalent (by 2) to P→(¬Q→R) is equivalent (by 1) to P→(Q∨R).

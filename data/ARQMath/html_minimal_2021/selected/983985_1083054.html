The cosine integral is defined by Ci⁢(x)=-∫x∞cos⁡tt⁢𝑑t.

Using this definition, ∫x∞cos⁡a⁢tt⁢𝑑t=∫a⁢x∞cos⁡uu⁢𝑑u=-Ci⁢(a⁢x).

Therefore,

∫0∞cos⁡b⁢x-cos⁡a⁢xx⁢𝑑xamp;=limϵ→0+(∫ϵ∞cos⁡b⁢xxdx-∫ϵ∞cos⁡a⁢xxdx)amp;=limϵ→0+(-Ci(bϵ)+Ci(aϵ))amp;=limϵ→0+(-ln(bϵ)+ln(aϵ)+𝒪(ϵ2))amp;=limϵ→0+(ln(ab)+𝒪(ϵ2))amp;=ln(ab).

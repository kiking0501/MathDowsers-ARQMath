calculus real-analysis complex-analysis
e-π⁢x2 does *not* have compact support.
The integral is finite, just change variable to y=x2 and apply the "improper integral" version of [Dirichlet's test](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dirichlet%27s_test).

Your integral is a slight variation of the improper Fresnel integrals.

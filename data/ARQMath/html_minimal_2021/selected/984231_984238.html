As these powers are infinite and rk∈{0,1,…,n-1}, then there exist k1,k2, such that rk1=rk2, and hence n∣10k2-10k1=10k2-k1⁢(10k1-1), and thus 10k2-k1⁢(10k1-1)=n⁢ℓ, and hence mn=110k2-k1⋅ℓ⁢m10k1-1.

The number of lattice paths from (0,0) to (n+1,n) consisting of (1,0)-steps and (0,1)-steps is (2⁢n+1n)=(2⁢n+1n+1) since we have to choose precisely n+1 (1,0)-steps out of 2⁢n+1 steps.

                               

Each of these paths crosses the vertical line x=n at a specific height k with 0≤k≤n. There are (n+kk) paths crossing the vertical line x=n at height k. Since this holds for each 0≤k≤n

we conclude

\begin{align*} \color{blue}{\sum_{k=0}^n\binom{n+k}{k}=\binom{2n+1}{n}} \end{align*}

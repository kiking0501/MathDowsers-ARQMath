Since limn→+∞⁡xn=0 and (xn)n∈ℕ∗ is a sequence of positive real numbers, note that :

xn1+xn⁢∼n→+∞xn

Therefore, the series ∑n∈ℕ∗xn1+xn and ∑n∈ℕ∗xn have the same behavior. Since ∑n∈ℕ∗xn is divergent,∑n∈ℕ∗xn1+xn diverges too.

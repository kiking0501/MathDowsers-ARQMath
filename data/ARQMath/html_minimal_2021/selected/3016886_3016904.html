We have that by f⁢(x)=∑n=0∞x3⁢n(3⁢n)!

f′⁢(x)=dd⁢x⁢∑n=0∞x3⁢n(3⁢n)!=∑n=1∞x3⁢n-1(3⁢n-1)!

f′′⁢(x)=dd⁢x⁢∑n=1∞x3⁢n-1(3⁢n-1)!=∑n=1∞x3⁢n-2(3⁢n-2)!

f′′′⁢(x)=dd⁢x⁢∑n=1∞x3⁢n-2(3⁢n-2)!=∑n=1∞x3⁢n-3(3⁢n-3)!=f⁢(x)

and f′′′⁢(x)=f⁢(x) has solution

f⁢(x)=c1⁢ex+c2⁢e-x/2⁢cos⁡(3⁢x2)+c3⁢e-x/2⁢sin⁡(3⁢x2)

with the initial conditions f⁢(0)=1, f′⁢(0)=0, f′′⁢(0)=0.

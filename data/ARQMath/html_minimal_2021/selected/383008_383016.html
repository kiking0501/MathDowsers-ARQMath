Motivation for creation of complex exponentiation

I am curious how mathematicians came to develop complex exponentiation. How is the rule for complex exponentiation derived?

complex-analysis definition exponentiation motivation exponential-function

It comes from the necessity to extend real exponentiation to the complex plane, so as to obtain a holomorphic function. So take real exponentiation series expansion ex=∑n=0+∞xnn! and put complex variable z in place of x. What you get is an entire function (i.e holomorphic in the whole plane) which trivially coincides with real exponentiation when restricted to the real axis. Moreover, it is unique: another holomorphic extension of ex would coincide with it on the real axis, so that it would be the same function by identity principle.

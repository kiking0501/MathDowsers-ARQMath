The equality case in the Schur's inequality follows from the proof.

For example, let t>0 and x, y and z are non-negatives.

Since our inequality is symmetric, we can assume x≥y≥z.

Thus, ∑c⁢y⁢cxt⁢(x-y)⁢(x-z)≥xt⁢(x-y)⁢(x-z)+yt⁢(y-x)⁢(y-z)= =(x-y)⁢(xt⁢(x-z)-yt⁢(y-z))≥0. The equality occurs for zt⁢(z-x)⁢(z-y)=0 and for (x-y)⁢(xt⁢(x-z)-yt⁢(y-z))=0. Now, easy to see that the equality occurs for x=y=z or for x=y, z=0 and for cyclic permutations of the last.

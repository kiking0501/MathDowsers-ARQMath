Here is my solution:

Let 5n-1 be divisible by 4. It means that 5n-1=4⁢k for some integer k, our base step is at n=0 and is clearly true.

We must prove if 5n-1=4⁢k then 5n+1-1=4⁢j.

5⁢(5n-1)=4⁢(5⁢k) whcih is equivalent to 5n+1-1=4⁢(5⁢k+1), therefore j=5⁢k+1.

Prove by induction that 5n-1 is divisible by 4.

Clearly 25 is not divisible by 3, so either its predecessor or successor is divisible by 3.

That is, either 25-1 or 25+1 is divisible by 3, whence their product is, as well.

Okay: Their product is 210-1, which we have now established is divisible by 3.

This number is still divisible by 3 after being doubled, and still divisible by 3 when we add 3 to it.

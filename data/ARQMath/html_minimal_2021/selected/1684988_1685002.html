Its easier to solve this question using parametric points.

Let one vertex of the rectangle be (a⁢cos⁡θ,b⁢sin⁡θ).

The other vertices are (a⁢cos⁡θ,-b⁢sin⁡θ), (-a⁢cos⁡θ,b⁢sin⁡θ), (-a⁢cos⁡θ,-b⁢sin⁡θ)

The area of rectangle formed is A⁢(θ)=4⁢a⁢b⁢cos⁡θ⁢sin⁡θ=2⁢a⁢b⁢sin⁡2⁢θ

Maximum area is 2⁢a⁢b and it occurs when θ=π4 (or when sin⁡2⁢θ is maximum).

When θ=π4, x-coordinate =a⁢cos⁡π4=a2

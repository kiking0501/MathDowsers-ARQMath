We have ϕ⁢(n)=p1α1-1⁢p2α2-1⁢⋯⁢pkαk-1⁢(p1-1)⁢(p2-1)⁢⋯⁢(pk-1) if n=p1α1⁢p2α2⋅pkαk with primes p1<p2<…<pk and αi∈ℕ. If ϕ⁢(n)=14 then one of the factors on the right must be divisible by 7, that is we have pi=7 ocurring with αi≥2; or there is some pi≡1(mod7). In the first case, we obtain that ϕ⁢(n) is a multiple of 71⋅(7-1)=42. In the latter case ϕ⁢(n) is a multiple of pi-1, where pi is a prime ≡1(mod7), hence pi≥29. In bothe cases, we see ϕ⁢(n)>14, contradiction.

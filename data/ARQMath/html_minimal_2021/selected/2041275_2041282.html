Notation: how to denote m-th element of a subsequence xnk

Sorry for probably stupid question, but I was not able to find an answer online. I want to denote m-th element of a subsequence xnk while still showing that it is subsequence xnk. Is there any notation for this - some sort of (xnk)m or {xnk}m or something like that?

sequences-and-series notation
Why not use xnm?

If n1,n2,n3,… is a strictly increasing sequence of positive integers, then xn1,xn2,xn3,…=(xnk) is a subsequence of x1,x2,x3,…=(xn). The mth term of (xnk) is denoted xnm.

In the case F=ℂ or F=ℝ:

First if A∈GLn⁢(F) then we have χA⁢B⁢(x)=det⁡(x⁢I-A⁢B)=det⁡(A⁢(x⁢I-B⁢A)⁢A-1)=det⁡(x⁢I-B⁢A)=χB⁢A⁢(x) Now by the density of GLn⁢(F) in ℳn⁢(F) and the continuity of the det function we have the desired result.

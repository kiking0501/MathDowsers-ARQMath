The standard Ramsey example is about edge coloring. This is about vertex coloring and much simpler: there must be at least 3 black vertices or at least 3 white vertices. Any three vertices in a complete graph combine to a K3.

there must be at least 3 black vertices or at least 3 white vertices. Any three vertices in a complete graph combine to a K3. -- Leen Droogendijk

Note that the above remains true if K6 is replaced with K5.

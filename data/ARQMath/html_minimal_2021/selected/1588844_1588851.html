The word "vector" means different things in different contexts. Sometimes "vector" means an ordered n-tuple of numbers. Sometimes "vector" means an equivalence class of directed line segments. Sometimes "vector" simply means "an element of a vector space" that we are working with. Hopefully context will make it clear which meaning of the word "vector" is being used.

What is your definition of the GCD and the LCM of two numbers?

Given two numbers, a,b, let d:=gcd⁡(a,b) and then f:=a/d and g:=b/d, giving a=d⁢f and b=d⁢g.

Now we know that the least common multiple, c, of a and b has to be divisible by d⁢f and by d⁢g. We also know that f and g have no common factors (otherwise d would be bigger by that factor). So c=d⁢f⁢g is the smallest number that can fulfill all these requirements.

Then a⁢b=d⁢f⋅d⁢g=d⋅d⁢f⁢g=gcd⋅lcm

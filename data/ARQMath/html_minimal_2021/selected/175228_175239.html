Let B be the matrix with all -1′⁢s. Since the r⁢a⁢n⁢k⁢(B)=1, it follows that λ=0 is an eigenvalue of B of multiplicity n-1.

Also, it is trivial to see that λ=-n is an eigenvalue of B, since in this case the rows add to 0. This proved that the characteristic polynomial of B is

det⁡(x⁢I-B)=xn-1⁢(x+n).

Set x=n-1 and you are done....

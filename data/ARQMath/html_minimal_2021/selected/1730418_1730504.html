Multiplying the LHS for an holomorphic test function f⁢(z), which then satisfies ∂z¯⁡f⁢(z)=0, and integrating over the unit disc D, we get ∬Df⁢(z)⁢∂z¯⁡(1z-w)⁡d⁢z∧d⁢z¯-2⁢iwhere in the second and third equalities we used respectively Stokes' theorem and Cauchy's equality.

Thus ∂z¯⁡(1z-w)=-π⁢δ⁢(z-w)

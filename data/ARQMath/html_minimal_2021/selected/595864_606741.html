Lemma: If gcd⁡(n,k)=1 then gcd⁡(n,n-k)=1.

Proof: Suppose d|n,d|n-k so d|n-(n-k) and d|k. So we have d|k and d|n and according to the first part, d=1. Thus, gcd⁡(n,n-k)=1.

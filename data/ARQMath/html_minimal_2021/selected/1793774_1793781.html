Moreover, bk+1=bk+ak+1>bk. So, (bk)k∈ℕ is an increasing sequence, and it converges, it converges to sup⁡({bk}). So, bk≤ex, for all k. But, then we have bk<bk+1≤ex, giving bk<ex, for all k.

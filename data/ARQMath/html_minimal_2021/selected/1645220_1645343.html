Which definition of e are you allowed to use?

For the first one, (1+xn)n<ex⇔1+xn<ex/n and this follows from ex≥1+x with equality only if x=0.

For the second one, ex<(1-xn)-n⇔ex/n<11-xn and this follows from ex<11-x if 0<x<1. This, in turn, follows from ex=∑n=0∞xnn! and 11-x=∑n=0∞xn if 0<x<1.

There is a way without using arctan.

Let the square root be a+b⁢i. Then, a2+2⁢a⁢b⁢i-b2=-5-12⁢i, so a2-b2=-5⁢(eq. 1) and 2⁢a⁢b⁢i=-12⁢i⁢(eq. 2);a⁢b=-6⁢(eq. 3).

From equation 3, a=-6b. Substituting into equation 1, (-6b)2-b2+5=0, and so:

36b2-b2+5=0 ⇒b4-5⁢b2-36=0 ⇒(b2-9)⁢(b2+4)=0 b2=9;b2=-4 b=±⁢3

Substiuting b=3 into equation 1, we have a=2; and substituting b=-3 we also get a=2. Therefore, the two solutions are 2+3⁢i and 2-3⁢i.

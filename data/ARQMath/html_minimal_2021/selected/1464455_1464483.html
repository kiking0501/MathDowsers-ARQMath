Assume that 52⁢k-1-32⁢k-1-22⁢k-1=15⁢m for some integer m.

Then 52⁢k+1-32⁢k+1-22⁢k+1=25⋅52⁢k-1-9⋅32⁢k-1-4⋅22⁢k-1

=21⋅52⁢k-1-5⋅32⁢k-1+4⁢(15⁢m)

Now each term has a factor of a 5 and a 3, so the number is divisible by 15.

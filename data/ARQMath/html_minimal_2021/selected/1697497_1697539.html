If you know the sum of a geometric series, you can differentiate both sides...it will look close to what you need...

The trick is to eliminate the k in the sum by integrating:

∑k=1nk⁢akamp;=a∑k=1nkak-1amp;=a∑k=1ndd⁢a(∫kak-1da)amp;=add⁢a(∑k=1n∫kak-1da)amp;=add⁢a(∑k=1n(ak+h))amp;=add⁢a(an+1-aa-1+hn)amp;=a(n⁢an+1-n⁢an-an+1(a-1)2)amp;=n⁢an+2-n⁢an+1-an+1+a(a-1)2

Changing the order of summations.
To intuit the change of order, draw a grid [1,n]×[1,n] and see which grid points are implicated in each case. In the first, we see that as j goes from 1 to i and j goes from 1 to n, the same points are covered when i goes from j to n and j goes from 1 to n.
It is indeed a double summation. Interestingly, we can pursue evaluating the double sum in closed form quite easily.

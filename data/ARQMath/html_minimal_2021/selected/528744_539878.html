I've done this here before, but it's quicker to write it again than to try to find it.

Let y=r⁢x. xy=yx becomes xr⁢x=(r⁢x)x or xr=r⁢x or xr-1=r or x=r1/(r-1) and y=r⁢x=r1+1/(r-1)=rr/(r-1).

If r=1, all values of x work since the condition is xr-1=r.

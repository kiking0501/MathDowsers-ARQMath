Let A be the event that we get two consecutive heads for the first time. E⁢(A)=12⁢(E⁢(A/F⁢i⁢r⁢s⁢t⁢t⁢o⁢s⁢s⁢w⁢a⁢s⁢a⁢t⁢a⁢i⁢l)+1)+12⁢(12⁢(E⁢(A/S⁢e⁢c⁢o⁢n⁢d⁢t⁢o⁢s⁢s⁢w⁢a⁢s⁢a⁢t⁢a⁢i⁢l)+2)+12*2)

Now, E⁢(A/F⁢i⁢r⁢s⁢t⁢T⁢o⁢s⁢s⁢w⁢a⁢s⁢a⁢t⁢a⁢i⁢l)=E⁢(A/S⁢e⁢c⁢o⁢n⁢d⁢t⁢o⁢s⁢s⁢w⁢a⁢s⁢a⁢t⁢a⁢i⁢l)=E⁢(A), because getting a tail puts us back on square one, and we have to 'start over'

Hence, E⁢(A)=12⁢(E⁢(A)+1)+12⁢(12⁢(E⁢(A)+2)+12*2)

E⁢(A)=6

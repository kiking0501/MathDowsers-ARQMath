plug in m=d⁢yd⁢x=-(x+5)y-9 you get

y=-(x+5)⁢(x-8)y-9-7⇒(y+7)⁢(y-9)=-(x+5)⁢(x-8)

so your answer will be the solution of the following system ..

(y+7)⁢(y-9)+(x+5)⁢(x-8)=0(x+5)2+(y-9)2=25

you just have to expand both equations and subtract them to cancel all the quadratic terms and you will be left with the equation of a line.

The solutions to your problem will be the points of intersection between that line and the circle (x+5)2+(y-9)2=25

@zcahfg2 \\ Whenever you have equations of two circles E=0 and F=0, the family of all circles that happen to pass through both intersections of the original circles is a⁢E+b⁢F=0, where a and b are non-zero quantities. When a and b are selected so that the degree-2 quantities vanish, then the resulting curve is the degenerate conic known as -- THE STRAIGHT LINE!

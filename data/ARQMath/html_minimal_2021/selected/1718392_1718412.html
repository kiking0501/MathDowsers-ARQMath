Here's my quick and dirty addition to the diagram.

If you consider a thin path between the concentric circles, then you can complete the entire contour Γ=Γ1+Γ2+Γ3+Γ4 and have it equal CR⁢1+CR⁢2 upon integration.

Then by the deformation of contours, you can shrink the integration on Γ down to the small circle about the singularity Cϵ.

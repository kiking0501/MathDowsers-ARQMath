The Riemann integral way is nice too, but if you insist on taking log you could apply Stolz-Cesaro Theorem too:

limn→∞⁡-n⁢log⁡n+∑k=1nlog⁡(k+n)n=limn→∞⁡(-(n+1)⁢log⁡(n+1)+∑k=1n+1log⁡(k+n))-(-n⁢log⁡n+∑k=1nlog⁡(k+n))=limn→∞⁡log⁡2+log⁡2⁢n+1n+1-n⁢log⁡(1+1n)=log⁡4-1

Giving you the desired limit 4e.

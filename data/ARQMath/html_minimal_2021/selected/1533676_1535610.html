Observe that f⁢(x)=11+279x and f⁢(3-x)=11+9x27. Adding these two expressions gives f⁢(x)+f⁢(3-x)=1. (It may be easier to see this by making a substitute such as a=279x and a-1=9x27). Therefore, S=f⁢(19)+f⁢(29)+⋯+f⁢(269)=13.

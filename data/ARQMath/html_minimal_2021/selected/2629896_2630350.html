Consider a row of 2⁢n+1 boxes, and colour n of them black. Suppose that the longest string of black boxes on the right has k boxes. Then the remaining n-k black boxes are among the first (2⁢n+1)-(k+1)=2⁢n-k boxes on the left.

This makes a one-to-one correspondence between (2⁢n+1n) and ∑k=0n(2⁢n-kn-k); the change of variables k′=n-k transforms the latter into ∑k′=0n(n+k′k′).

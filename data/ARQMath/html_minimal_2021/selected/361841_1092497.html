Step One: 11=1, 212>1, suppose n>1⟹n1n>11n=1.

Step Two: Define αn=n1n-1. By the Binomial Formula for every index n>2, n=(1+αn)n=1+n⁢αn+n⁢(n-1)2⁢αn2+⋯>1+n⁢(n-1)2⁢αn2, since αn>0. n>1+n⁢(n-1)2⁢αn2⟹1>n2⁢αn2⟹2n>αn2 and by the comparison lemma and since the product of convergent sequences converges to the product of the limits, you can see that limn→∞⁡αn=0.

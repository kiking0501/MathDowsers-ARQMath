There are also two additional methods.

Method 1: There is an necessary condition that states that a series of positive and decreasing an can only be convergent if limn→∞⁡n⋅an=0.

Method 2: Use Raabe–Duhamel's test (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Convergence_tests)

The first one is quite nice.

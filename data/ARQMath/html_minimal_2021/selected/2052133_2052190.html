Let I be the integral given by

I=∫0π11-cos⁡(β)⁢sin⁡(x)⁢𝑑x

where β≠2⁢ℓ⁢π for integer ℓ.

Enforcing the substitution x→x+π/2 and exploiting symmetry reveals

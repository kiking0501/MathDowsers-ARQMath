The sum S=∑i=1n12i=1-12n is geometric, thus easy to calculate.

Here is a simple elementary way of calculating

T=∑i=1ni2i.:

T=∑i=1ni2i=12+∑i=2ni2i=12-n+12n+1+∑i=2n+1i2i.

Changing the index in the last sum yields:

T=2n-n-12n+1+∑i=1ni+12i+1=2n-n-12n+1+∑i=1ni2i+1+∑i=1n12i+1.

Thus

T=2n-n-12n+1+12⁢T+12-12n+1

Thus

12⁢T=2n+1-n-22n+1.

Hence

∑i=1ni2i=2n+1-n-22n.

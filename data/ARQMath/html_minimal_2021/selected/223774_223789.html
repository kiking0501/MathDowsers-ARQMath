See this post from a day ago; it discusses a formula for the length of a side of any given 2n-gon inscribed in a circle of radisus R. So you'd use R = 1 for a unit circle. (Note: this only helps with regular polygons with an even number of sides, and is recursive, so it might help to put Andre's info (comment above) and Ross's answer to work!) http://math.stackexchange.com/questions/222325/relationship-between-the-sides-of-inscribed-polygons

Hint: The sides of the regular n-gon are chords of the circle, with central angle 2⁢πn. The diagonals are also chords, with central angles some multiple of that. Think about how many diagonals come from one vertex.

Rewrite the product as

∏k=1n(2⁢k-1)=(2⁢n)!2n⁢n!

Then use the Stirling approximation

n!∼nne-n2⁢π⁢n (n→∞)

to deduce that the asymptotic behavior of the ratio, which is

(2⁢n)!22⁢n⁢n!⁢nn

behaves as 2⁢e-n as n→∞. Therefore, the limit is zero.

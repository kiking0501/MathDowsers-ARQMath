You proceed as in the case with 1+x2 in the denominator. Define f⁢(z)=ei⁢z1+z6 and integrate along a semicircle of radius R on the upper half-plane, use the residue theorem, and let R→∞. The poles go f are the solutions of the equation 1+z6=0.

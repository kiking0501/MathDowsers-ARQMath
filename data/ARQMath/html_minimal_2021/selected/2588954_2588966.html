Why is log⁡(-1)=1.36⁢i instead of π⁢i?

According to Google, log⁡(-1)=1.36⁢i. Why is that true? Euler's identity says ei⁢π=-1. Taking the log on both sides gives log⁡(-1)=i⁢π(≠1.36⁢i).

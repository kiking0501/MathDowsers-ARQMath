R is not reflexive, but your reasoning is off. For any non-negative integer, we have that x⁢\gt⁢2⁢x. Hence, in those cases, (x,x)∉R.

R is not symmetric, since because 3⁢\gt⁢2⋅1=2, (3,1)∈R, but since 1⁢\gt⁢2⋅3=6,(1,3)∉R.

However, R is transitive. If x⁢\gt⁢2⁢y and y⁢\gt⁢2⁢z, then x⁢\gt⁢2⁢y⇔x⁢\gt⁢2⁢(2⁢z), so certainly, 12⁢x⁢\gt⁢2⁢z⟹x⁢\gt⁢2⁢z.

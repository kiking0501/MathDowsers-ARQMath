A group of order 6 is isomorphic to C6 or S3. We can rule out C6 because A4 has no element of order 6; therefore the subgroup must be isomorphic to S3. But S3 has three elements of order 2. In A4 the three elements of order 2 generate a group of order 4, so they can't be contained in a group of order 6.

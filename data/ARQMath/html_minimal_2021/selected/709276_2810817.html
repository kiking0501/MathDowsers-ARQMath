Sn=n2⁢(2⁢a1+(n-1)⁢d) 689=n2⁢(2⁢(-7)+10⁢(n-1)) 689=n2⁢(10⁢n-24) 689=5⁢n2-12⁢n 0=5⁢n2-12⁢n-689 0=(n-13)⁢(5⁢n+53) n={13,-535}

Since the number of terms cannot be negative nor fractional, you can eliminate the second solution and get that n is 13.

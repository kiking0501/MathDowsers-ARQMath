If the polar form of z is z=r⁢(cos⁡θ+i⁢sin⁡θ), there are n distinct solutions to the equation wn=z: w=rn⁢(cos⁡θ+2⁢π⁢kn+i⁢sin⁡θ+2⁢π⁢kn), where k=0,1,…,n-1. In your case, z=i, whose polar form is given by r=1, θ=π/2.

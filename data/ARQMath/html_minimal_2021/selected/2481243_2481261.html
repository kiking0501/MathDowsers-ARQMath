Which is equivalent to

(α-2)3+(β-2)3+(α-2)+(β-2)=0

Let α-2=a and β-2=b

Now we've ;

a3+b3+a+b=0

(a+b)⁢(a2-a⁢b+b2+1)=0

This give us

a+b=0⟹\color⁢b⁢l⁢u⁢e⁢α+β=4

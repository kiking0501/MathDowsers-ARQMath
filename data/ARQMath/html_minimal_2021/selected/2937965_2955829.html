4⁢x4+1=(2⁢x2+2⁢x+1)⁢(2⁢x2-2⁢x+1).

(22⁢k+1+2k+1+1)⁢(22⁢k+1-2k+1+1)=24⁢k+2+1.

xp+1|x2⁢k+1+1,

which applied at x=4 gives

P-1=(x1x12+y12,y1x12+y12).

so 22⁢p+1 is not our first factor, and, as our first factor is prime, it cannot share any common factors with 22⁢p+1. This means that

22⁢p+1|22⁢k+1-2k+1+1.

As 2⁢k+1=m⁢p for some odd m=2⁢n+1, we have that

22⁢k+1=2m⁢p≡22⁢n⁢p+p≡(-1)n⁢2pmod(22⁢p+1),

and similarly

2k+1=2(2⁢n+1)⁢p+12=2n⁢p+(p+12)≡2p+12⁢Kmod(22⁢p+1),

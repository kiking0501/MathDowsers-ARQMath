Actually, it may be better to modify the inequality so that you are comparing to 0: n3-2⁢n-1⁢\gt⁢0. Then you can apply subtraction and verify that step n+1 is greater than step n.

Hint: For the inductive step you can assume that k3-2⁢k-1>0 for some k∈ℕ such that k≥2, then for k+1

(k+1)3-2⁢(k+1)-1=⋯>0

(in ⋯ you should use the hypothesis, k3-2⁢k-1>0) because for k≥2, 3⁢k⁢(k+1)≥2 so 3⁢k⁢(k+1)-1≥1>0.

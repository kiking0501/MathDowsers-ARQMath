I=∫01Li2⁡(x)1-x2⁢𝑑x,

where Li2 is the dilogarithm function.

A numerical approximation of it is

I≈1.39130720750676668181096483812551383015419528634319581297153⁢…

As Lucian said I has the following equivalent forms:

I=∫01Li2⁡(x)1-x2⁢𝑑x=∫01Li2⁡(x)2⁢x⁢1-x⁢𝑑x=∫0π2Li2⁡(sin⁡x)⁢𝑑x=∫0π2Li2⁡(cos⁡x)⁢𝑑x

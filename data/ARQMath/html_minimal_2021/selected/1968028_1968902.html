Hence

cos⁡π5⁢cos⁡2⁢π5=14

And

cos⁡π5⁢cos⁡2⁢π5=12⁢(cos⁡π5+cos⁡3⁢π5)=14

So, you have the system

{(1)1(2)2

Hence cos⁡π5 and cos⁡3⁢π5 are the roots of x2-12⁢x-14. For this trinomial, Δ=54 and

x=1±54

Since cos⁡π5>0 and cos⁡3⁢π5<0, this means

cos⁡π5=1+54

And

cos⁡2⁢π5=-cos⁡3⁢π5=5-14

Then

sin⁡π10=sin⁡(π2-2⁢π5)=cos⁡2⁢π5=5-14

cos⁡π10=1+cos⁡π52=5+58

Next

sin⁡π12=sin⁡(π4-π6)=22⁢32-12⁢22=24⁢(3-1)

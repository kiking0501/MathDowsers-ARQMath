I just want to extend J.M.'s solution to a full solution of the exercise:

∫0xsin⁡(t)t⁢𝑑t

Is with Maclaurin expansion (sin⁡t=t⁢(1-t2/3!+t4/5!-t6/7!+⋯)) equals to

∫0xtt⁢(1-t23!+t45!-t67!+⋯)⁢𝑑t=∫0x(1-t23!+t45!-t67!+⋯)⁢𝑑t

We do now integrate this series to:

∫0x(1-t23!+t45!-t67!+⋯)⁢𝑑t=x-x33*3!+x55*5!-x77*7!+⋯

Above we did use the flowing fact: ∫0xtk⁢𝑑t=tk+1k+1|0x=xk+1k+1-0k+1k+1=xk+1k+1

We can now write the result as a series: x-x33*3!+x55*5!-x77*7!+⋯=∑n=0∞(-1)n⁢x(2⁢n+1)(2⁢n+1)*(2⁢n+1)!=S⁢i⁢(x)

And see that we get the Sine Integral.

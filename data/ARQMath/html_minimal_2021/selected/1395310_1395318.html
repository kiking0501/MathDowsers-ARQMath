Note that a⁢b=a⁢b is true only for a≥0 or b≥0. On the other hand, if a,b<0, then a⁢b=(-a)⁢(-b)=-a⁢-b a⁢b=i⁢-a⋅i⁢-b=--a⁢-b

Therefore the formula a=ab⁢b holds if and only if b≠0 and (ab≥0 or b≥0) if and only if a⁢b>0 or b>0. In your second proof, a=1,b=-1 does not satisfy the above condition.

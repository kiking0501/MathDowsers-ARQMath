If you are interested in inclusion exclusion try this. Our universe will be 01 strings of length n. We will double count the number of ways to get all 0. The LHS is clear there is only one way to do this. For the RHS, let Ai be the event that the it⁢h spot has a 1 in it. This should set you up for PIE. So we should have

1=∑S⊂[n](-1)|S|⁢|⋂i∈SAi|

I leave it to you to compute |⋂i∈SAi|. But I think the binomial theorem is much easier as shown in the other answers.

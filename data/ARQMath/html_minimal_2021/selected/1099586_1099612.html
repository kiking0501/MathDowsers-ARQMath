Presumably this is subject to some constraint, say, all of the summands being positive.
If we are talking about non-negative numbers, through the AM-GM inequality.

This is a consequence of the A.M.-G.M. inequality, if the numbers are non negative.

Let a+b+c=3⁢k. Then, by A.M. G.M. inequality, we get,

(a⁢b⁢c)1/3≤k, and so, a⁢b⁢c≤k3. Also the equality is obtained if and only if a=b=c, which can be easily seen to be case from the proof of A.M.-G.M. inequality for instance.

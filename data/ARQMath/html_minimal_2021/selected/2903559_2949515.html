Another way to solve the Pell equation in Pell’s equation without irrational numbers by Norman Wildberger.

Example find smallest solution in pari/gp:

njw(d)= {  L= [1,0;1,1]; R= [1,1;0,1];  A= [1,0;0,-d]; Q= A; N= 1;  while(1,   a= Q[1,1]; b= Q[1,2]; c= Q[2,2];   t= a+2*b+c;   if(t<0, Q= R~*Q*R; N= N*R, Q= L~*Q*L; N= N*L);   if(Q==A, break())  );  return(N[,1]~) };  ? njw(119) %2 = [120, 11]

We give a probabilistic proof, so not an answer to the question. We want to show that ∑k=0n(2⁢n+1k)⁢122⁢n+1=12.

Alicia throws 2⁢n+1 fair coins onto a coffee table. She will be happy if she sees ≤n heads. The probability she will be happy is the left-hand side of (1).

The coffee table is made of glass, and Beti is under the table (don't ask!). Beti will be happy if she sees ≤n tails.

It is clear that Beti will be happy if and only if Alicia is not. But by symmetry their probabilities of being happy are the same, so each is equal to 12.

No, you were right. That does not hold. One does not 'distribute' square roots. And no, even when 'simplifying,' one cannot distribute square roots in general. The bigger idea is that while in real numbers, we can say 4=2, this only makes sense when we can distinguish between the positive and the negative answer. We can't do so in the complex numbers.

In particular, you sequence is simply an obfuscation of the fact that both i and -i square to give -1.

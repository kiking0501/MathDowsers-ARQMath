The property you just asked is the Vandermonde's identity.

Vandermonde's Identity states that ∑k=0r(mk)⁢(nr-k)=(m+nr) which can be proven combinatorially by noting that any combination of r objects from a group of m+n objects must have some 0≤k≤r objects from group m and the remaining from group n.

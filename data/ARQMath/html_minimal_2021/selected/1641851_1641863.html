Hint Since x↦log⁡x is a strictly increasing function, applying it to both sides of the inequality gives that it is equivalent to x≥e⁢log⁡x.

There are at least two options here:

(1) Rearrange the inequality as log⁡xx≤1e, and analyze the expression on the l.h.s. of this equality. In particular, show that its maximum value is (at most) 1e.

(2) From inspecting the original equation, we know that both sides of the inequality are equal at x=e. So, if the inequality holds we must have that the derivatives of both sides of the inequality agree at that point, and indeed, this is straightforward to check. These facts, together with d2d⁢x2⁢log⁡x⁢&⁢l⁢t;0=d2d⁢x2⁢x are sufficient to give the inequality.

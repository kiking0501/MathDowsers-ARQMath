The locus of points that see an ellipse under a right angle is a circle having radius a2+b2:

Since the tangent to the ellipse in the point P=(a⁢cos⁡θ,b⁢sin⁡θ) is given by: P+λ⁢(-a⁢sin⁡θ,b⁢cos⁡θ), by solving: a2⁢(cos⁡θ-λ⁢sin⁡θ)2+b2⁢(sin⁡θ+λ⁢cos⁡θ)2=a2+b2 with respect to λ we find two vertices of a circumscribed rectangle associated with P (and the other two vertices are simply given by the symmetric with respect to the origin). Now our min/max problem depends just on θ.

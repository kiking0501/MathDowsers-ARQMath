We need 3⁢sec⁡π5+tan⁡π30=cot⁡2⁢π15=tan⁡11⁢π30 as 2⁢π15+11⁢π30=π2

⇔3⁢sec⁡π5=tan⁡11⁢π30-tan⁡π30

⇔3cos⁡π5=sin⁡(11⁢π30-π30)cos⁡π30⁢cos⁡11⁢π30

As sin⁡(11⁢π30-π30)=sin⁡π3=32,

⇔cos⁡π5=2⁢cos⁡π30⁢cos⁡11⁢π30=cos⁡π3+cos⁡2⁢π5

⇔cos⁡π5-cos⁡2⁢π5=cos⁡π3=12

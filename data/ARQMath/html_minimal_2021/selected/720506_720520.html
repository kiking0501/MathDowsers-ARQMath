Hand wavey approach: Suppose the sequence xn does converge some to some limit, say ℓ, as n→∞. Taking the limit of both sides of the recurrence relation xn+1=xn+1xn, we get

ℓ=ℓ+1ℓ⇔0=1ℓ.

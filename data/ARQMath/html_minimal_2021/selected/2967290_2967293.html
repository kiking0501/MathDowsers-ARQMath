The convergence of a r.v. moment of order r, about a and its central moments

r>0, E⁢[|X|r]<∞ iff E⁢[|X-a|r]<∞ for every a.
It is trivial when r=2, but how could I proof the statement with the other values of r?

probability measure-theory expected-value

|X-a|r≤(|X|+|a|)r≤(2⁢max⁡{|X|,|a|})r=2r⁢max⁡{|X|r,|a|r}≤2r⁢(|X|r+|a|r) so E⁢|X-a|r≤2r⁢(E⁢|X|r+|a|r); similar argument in the other direction.

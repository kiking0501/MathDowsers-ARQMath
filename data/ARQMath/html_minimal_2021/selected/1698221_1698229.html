You request ∑j=1nj⁢aj. Then we have \begin{align*} \sum_{j=1}^n j a^j &= a \sum_{j=1}^n j a^{j-1} \\ &= a \sum_{j=1}^n \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}a} a^j \\ &= a \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}a} \sum_{j=1}^n a^j \\ &= a \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}a} \frac{a^{n+1} - a}{a-1} \\ &= a \frac{n a^{n+1} - n a^n - a^n + 1}{(a-1)^2} \\ &= \frac{a^{n+1}(an - n -1) + a}{(a-1)^2} \text{.} \end{align*}

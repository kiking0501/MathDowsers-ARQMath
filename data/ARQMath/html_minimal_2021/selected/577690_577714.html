Because, since all limits of the form limn→∞⁡kn are 0, for all finite numbers k, then the product 0⋅∞ becomes meaningless. Sometimes it can even be infinity itself, since limn→∞⁡nn2=0, for instance. So it is best to avoid such expressions, especially if you are a beginner. Otherwise you'll soon be asking questions like why limn→∞⁡(1+1n)n=e instead of 1, since, by all appearances, 1∞=0, and limn→∞⁡1n=1.

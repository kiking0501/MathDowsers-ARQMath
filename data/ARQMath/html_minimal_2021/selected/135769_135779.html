The "right direction" will depend on the exact framework on which you are working. Do you have a list of axioms and a set of inference rules? (Which ones?) Do you have truth tables and are truth tables considered valid proofs of equivalence?

If you know how to construct truth tables (and if you know what "logically equivalent" means), that's one direction.

I wonder what the agenda here is, seems sinister.

A common calculus problem is to prove that the series 1+122+132+⋯ converges. More difficult is to find the sum:

1+122+132+⋯=π26.

Much more mysterious is the series:

1+123+133+⋯=1.2020569⁢…

After a few hundred years of searching, it seems unlikely that there is a simple closed form. This number is known as Apéry's constant or just ζ⁢(3).

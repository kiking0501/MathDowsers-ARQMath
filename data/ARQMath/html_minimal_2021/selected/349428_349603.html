Hint: Differentiate both sides of et⁢XT⁢B⁢et⁢X=B with respect to t and evaluate the derivatives at t=0, you get XT⁢B+B⁢X=0. So, all solutions to (1) must be solutions to (2). Conversely, by using the power series expansion of the matrix exponential function, show that whenever (2) is satisfied, (1) is satisfied too.

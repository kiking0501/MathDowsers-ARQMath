You're doing fine. They're not supposed to be equal in general.

It will usually be the case that ∂3⁡f∂⁡x2⁢∂⁡y≠∂3⁡f∂⁡y2⁢∂⁡x. For this particular function, f⁢(x,y)=sin⁡(x⁢y), the variables x and y play symmetrical roles. A consequence is that ∂3⁡f∂⁡x2⁢∂⁡y will differ from ∂3⁡f∂⁡y2⁢∂⁡x only in that x and y get interchanged.

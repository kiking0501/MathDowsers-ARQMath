Finding remainders using modulo

Determine the remainder of 20142015⋅20162017+20182019 divided by 13.

I can't figure out how to manipulate the 2018 part to get it to some form of 13. Any suggestions?

modular-arithmetic

We have: 20142015⋅20162017=122015⋅12017(mod13)=12⋅1(mod13)=12(mod13), and 20182019=32019=(312)168⋅33=1168⋅1=1(mod13). Adding these mods we have: S=(12+1)(mod13)=13(mod13)=0(mod13).

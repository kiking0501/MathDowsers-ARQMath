Think about, in general,

S=1+2⁢r+3⁢r2+4⁢r3+⋯

r⁢S=r+2⁢r2+3⁢r3+⋯

S-r⁢S=(1-r)⁢S=1+r+r2+r3+⋯=11-r

Therefore

S=1(1-r)2

This, however, is not really the series you listed; that series has an extra factor of r; so the sum is actually r/(1-r)2. Plug in r=1/2 and the sum is 2.

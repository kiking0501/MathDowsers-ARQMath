Do you know the steps to an induction proof?

The formula is true for the case n=1, since 1×2×3=6=1×2×3×44.

Now, suppose that the formula is true for n. We have that 1×2×3+⋯+n⁢(n+1)⁢(n+2)+(n+1)⁢(n+2)⁢(n+3)=n⁢(n+1)⁢(n+2)⁢(n+3)4+(n+1)⁢(n+2)⁢(n+3)=n⁢(n+1)⁢(n+2)⁢(n+3)4+4⁢(n+1)⁢(n+2)⁢(n+3)4=(n+1)⁢(n+2)⁢(n+3)⁢(n+4)4

So, since the n+1 case is true, the induction holds.

@barakmanos There is an [irrationality measure](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Liouville_number#Irrationality_measure) μ that deals with "degree of irrationality". Rational numbers have μ=1, algebraic irrationals have μ=2, and transcendentals have 2≤μ≤∞.

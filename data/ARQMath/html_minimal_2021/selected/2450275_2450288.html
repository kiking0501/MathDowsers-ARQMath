Solve for x: x(xn)=n

x(xn)=n . Solve for x. I tried using logarithms however I got stuck with n⁢log⁡(x⁢log⁡x)=log⁡(log⁡n). Can someone explain the solution to this equation?

Raising both sides to the nth power gives (xn)xn=nn, so xn=n and x=nn. For n<1, there's a second solution, though.

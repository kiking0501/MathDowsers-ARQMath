By a chain rule we have d⁢fd⁢x=d⁢fd⁢(x2)⋅d⁢(x2)d⁢x

If f⁢(x)=x, then we arrive at 1=d⁢(x)d⁢(x2)⋅2⁢x. Finally d⁢(x)d⁢(x2)=12⁢x for x≠0.

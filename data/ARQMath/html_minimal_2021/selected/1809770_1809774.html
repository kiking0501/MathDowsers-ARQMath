Method 1:-

From Euler Totient Function(or theorem) we know-

aϕ⁢(n)≡1(modn) for gcd⁡(a,n)=1

Here you have,220≡1(modn).

Comparing the two equations we have,ϕ⁢(n)=20.Try to find n from it.

Method 2:-

We know that for all n,(a-b)|(an-bn) and an-bn is also divisible by a+b if n is odd.

So,We need to find x such that x|220-1

Note that 220-1 can be written as 220-120.

This can be written in the following forms-

(25)4-(15)4 Hence,n=25-1 is a factor.

(210)2-(110)2 Hence,n=210-1 is a factor.

(24)5-(14)5 Hence,both 24-1 and 24+1 are factors.

Important note-Factors of the values of n given are also factors of the number.

Knowing the identity that cos⁡(2⁢a)=2⁢cos2⁡(a)-1 we can directly derive:

cos⁡(3⁢a)=4⁢cos3⁡(a)-3⁢cos⁡(a) and

cos⁡(4⁢a)=8⁢cos4⁡(a)-8⁢cos2⁡(a)+1

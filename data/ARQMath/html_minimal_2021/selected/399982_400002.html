Let's evaluate the following integral. Many trials but no success. ∫-ππsin⁡n⁢x(1+πx)⁢sin⁡x⁢𝑑x

sequences-and-series definite-integrals

Hint:

Fix x=-x⟹d⁢x=-d⁢x

I=-∫π-πsin⁡n⁢(-x)(1+(π)-x)⁢sin⁡(-x)⁢𝑑x

I=∫-ππsin⁡n⁢(-x)(1+(π)-x)⁢sin⁡(-x)⁢𝑑x

I=∫-πππx⁢sin⁡n⁢(x)(πx+1)⁢sin⁡x⁢𝑑x

Add this to I=∫-ππsin⁡n⁢x(1+πx)⁢sin⁡x⁢𝑑x

2⁢I=∫-ππsin⁡n⁢xsin⁡x⁢πx+11+πx⁢𝑑x

If you have ∫-aaf⁢(x)⁢𝑑x and f⁢(x) is even, the same integral can be written as 2⁢∫0af⁢(x)⁢𝑑x. I believe you can do the further caculations.

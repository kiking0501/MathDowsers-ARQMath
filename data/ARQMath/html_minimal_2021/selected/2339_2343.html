By a well know formula we have (cos⁡k⁢π2⁢n+i⁢sin⁡k⁢π2⁢n)2⁢n=(-1)k Hence by Binomial theorem we have ([x] is not an integer part, brackets are added for clarity) ∑t=02⁢n(2⁢nt)⁢[cos⁡k⁢π2⁢n]t⋅[i⁢sin⁡k⁢π2⁢n]2⁢n-t=(-1)k Now we consider only imaginary part of this: ∑r=0n-1(2⁢n2⁢r+1)⁢[cos⁡k⁢π2⁢n]2⁢r+1⋅[i⁢sin⁡k⁢π2⁢n]2⁢n-2⁢r-1=0 Divide it by [cos⁡k⁢π2⁢n]2⁢n: σ⁢(ϵ8) Now multiply by i⁢tan⁡k⁢π2⁢n: ∑r=0n-1(2⁢n2⁢r+1)⁢[i⁢tan⁡k⁢π2⁢n]2⁢n-2⁢r=0 So [tan⁡k⁢π2⁢n]2 are roots of the following polynomial: ∑r=0n-1(2⁢n2⁢r+1)⁢[-x]n-r=0 Hence by Vieta's_formulas sum of it roots is equal to (2⁢n3)(2⁢n1)=(2⁢n-1)⁢(n-1)3

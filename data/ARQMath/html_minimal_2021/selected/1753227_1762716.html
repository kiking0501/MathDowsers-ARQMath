It is not difficult to produce a map that maps the line y=0 onto some parabola, lines y=const onto parallel curves of the parabola, and lines x=const. onto lines intersecting the parabola orthogonally. It becomes difficult, however, if you insist that on the "soul" y=0 lengths are preserved. Things improve if you replace the parabola with an arc of a catenary.

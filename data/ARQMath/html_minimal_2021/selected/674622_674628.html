(1) is the arithmetic mean - harmonic mean inequality for 3 variables. (2) is true without the condition that a,b,c be positive (changing signs does not change the left side but can reduce the right side). Inequality (2) for all real values of a,b,c is the positive-definiteness of a quadratic form, which is equivalent to the ability to write the form as a sum of squares of linear forms, in this case ∑(a-b)2.
